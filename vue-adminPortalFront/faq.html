FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: What are the top 5 best selling books?

A: Wuthering Heights, Jane Eyre, The Odessy, Ulysses, The Great Gatsby.

Q: What is the best selling romance novel?

A: Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen (1813)- Estimated 30M+ Copies Sold.

Q: Please help me find a drama fiction book that takes place in England

A: "The Murder of Roger Ackroyd" by Agatha Christie - This classic novel features one of Christie's most famous detectives, Hercule Poirot, as he investigates the murder of a wealthy man in a small English village. "Murder on the Orient Express" by Agatha Christie - Another famous work by Agatha Christie, this novel follows Hercule Poirot as he solves a murder that occurs on a luxury train traveling through Europe. "The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo" by Stieg Larsson - While not exclusively set in England, part of this gripping thriller takes place in the UK. It follows journalist Mikael Blomkvist and hacker Lisbeth Salander as they delve into a decades-old disappearance and uncover dark secrets.
